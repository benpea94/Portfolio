About Me

I am a hard-working and passionate aspiring software engineer looking for an exciting new challenge. I have worked as a freelance musician and tutor for over eleven years and I am now looking for a new role to focus my passion and dedication.

I embarked upon my journey in Tech in October 2022 and have since completed a back-end engineering Bootcamp funded by The Department of Education in partnership with HyperionDev.

Since the completion of this course I have strengthened and diversified my skills by learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

In my spare time I still continue to play music professionally in a blues band, gigging regularly across the south of England.
